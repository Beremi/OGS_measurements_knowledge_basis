# Workplan And Collaboration

## HERMES Task Framing

The BGR contribution to HERMES Task 5 is centered on the Mont Terri CD-A experiment as a field-scale validation and method-development case.

BGR's December 2024 workplan input emphasizes:

- long-term measurements of deformation, humidity, water content, pore-water pressure, crack width, and fracture/heterogeneity characterization;
- point measurements and spatial measurements, including 2D ERT, TDR, and laser scans;
- OGS6 finite-element modelling for THM/TM/H couplings;
- measurement-driven modelling and ML analysis of EDZ and water-content evolution;
- combining data-driven modelling with classical process modelling;
- producing an improved understanding of EDZ evolution and a workflow for measurement-driven process modelling.

The plan distinguishes the HERMES work from earlier EURAD-I work by focusing on heterogeneous near-field clay-rock processes and explicit measurement-data integration.
