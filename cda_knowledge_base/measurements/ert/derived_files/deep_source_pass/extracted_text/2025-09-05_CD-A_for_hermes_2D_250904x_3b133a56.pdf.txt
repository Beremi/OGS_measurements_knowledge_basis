Taupe (TDR measurements)
ε_r = ε_rock * (1 - φ) + ε_w * φ * S_w
With: 
ε_r : residual apparent relative dielectric permittivity, 
ε_rock: apparent relative dielectric constant of rock, 
φ pores in the rock, 
ε_w: apparent relative dielectric constant of water, 
S_w: water saturation.
∆ε_r = ε_Gestein * (1 - φ) + ε_w * φ * ∆ S_w
ε_w (permittivity): ca. 80
φ = Porosity * 100 [Vol.%pores]
