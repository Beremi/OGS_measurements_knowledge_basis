Preliminary results
➢ NMR response of Opalinus powders very similar to Bentonite, esp. Na-Bentonite powder
➢ Cut-off values for interlayer water found to be at  ~0.5 ms to 0.6 ms
➢ Opa in natural solid formation: bimodal NMR response indicates the two types of water binding
➢ Separating them using cut-off values seem possible, however, diffusion and data noise smears the
water signal in the range of the cut-off => quantification still challenging!
